FAQ

"Can't I just ask ChatGPT?"

You can. It'll tell you your idea is interesting and suggest some improvements. It won't tell you you're solving a problem nobody has. It hasn't built 30 things and watched most of them fail. And it definitely can't look at your app and tell you why real users will bounce in 10 seconds.

"What if you tell me to kill my project?"

Then I saved you months of building something nobody wants. That's the most valuable outcome, even though it doesn't feel like it.

"I'm not a designer. Will I understand the UX feedback?"

That's exactly who this is for. I won't throw jargon at you. I'll show you specific things in your app and explain why they're losing users, in plain language, with concrete fixes you can implement yourself.

"Do I need to have already built something?"

Not required, but you'll get more value if you have. Even a rough prototype or mockup gives me something to react to. If you're at the pure idea stage, we'll focus on whether the idea is worth building at all before you invest more time.

"What if I disagree with your verdict?"

Good. You're buying my analysis and perspective, not a binding decision. But at minimum, you'll know exactly which assumptions you're betting on — and that's worth more than building in the dark.
