FAQ

"Can't I just ask ChatGPT?"

You can. It'll tell you your idea is interesting and give you a balanced analysis. It won't tell you you're wasting your time. It hasn't built 30 things and watched most of them fail. It can't read between the lines of your answers and spot the assumptions you don't even know you're making.

"What if you tell me to kill my project?"

Then I saved you months of building something nobody wants. That's the most valuable outcome, even though it doesn't feel like it.

"Do I need to have already built something?"

No. Idea stage is fine. But you should have at least thought about who it's for and what problem it solves. If you just have a vague notion, spend another week thinking before booking.

"What if I disagree with your verdict?"

That's fine. You're buying my analysis and perspective, not a binding decision. You might see something I don't. But at minimum, you'll know exactly which assumptions you're betting on.
